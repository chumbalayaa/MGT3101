About MGT 3101

Organizational Behavior is a class that dives into the study of people in the workplace. Specifically, this field strives to optimize, through study and application, Organizational Comittment and Job Performance.

What motivates a worker? How can we encourage workers to be a part of the company rather than just an employee? What factors can help create the perfect work environment and increase production?

You can learn about all these questions and more if you take MGT 3101!
